⬡ CH-01 — SCREEN TOUCH SENSOR

Tap ON to activate. Touch the screen during an investigation. The needle and readout respond to the pressure, area, and location of each touch. A spike in activity with no obvious cause may indicate a presence. The session touch count tracks all contacts since the last reset.

⬡ CH-02 — EMF DETECTOR

Uses your phone's built-in magnetometer to detect real electromagnetic field fluctuations in microteslas (μT) — the same principle as a K-II EMF meter used by professional investigators. The bar display and readout are colour-coded by deviation from your calibrated baseline:

■ AMBIENT   ■ ELEVATED   ■ HIGH   ■ ANOMALY

On entering a new location, tap ◎ CALIBRATE BASELINE to set the ambient field as your reference point. Any significant deviation from that baseline triggers an ANOMALY alert with a voice notification. The ΔEMF counter tracks events this session. X, Y, Z show the raw axis readings in μT.

● Live data — uses your device's real magnetometer hardware. Keep the phone away from metal objects and other electronics when calibrating for best results.

◈ GHOSTEC — WORD ENGINE

Tap ▶ INITIATE SEQUENCE to start the engine. It cycles through 5000 words drawn from a curated paranormal lexicon and speaks each one aloud. Listen for words that feel relevant to your location or questions — these may be meaningful responses. The engine stops automatically at the end of the sequence.

? — INVESTIGATIVE QUESTIONS

Found on both pages. Use these prompts to communicate during a session. Tap ▶ READ ALOUD to have the question spoken out loud. Tap NEXT QUESTION to cycle to the next prompt. Both pages always show the same question.

⊙ — RETURN TO HOME

Tap the Cryotrac logo in the top left at any time to return to the home screen. The ambient music will restart.

↺ — SESSION RESET

Tap the reset button in the top right to restart the session timer and clear all counters. Use this at the start of each new investigation location.
