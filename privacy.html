CRYOTRAC

PRIVACY POLICY — PARANORMAL INVESTIGATION TOOL

Effective date: April 2026

1. OVERVIEW

Cryotrac ("the App") is a paranormal investigation tool designed for entertainment purposes. This Privacy Policy explains what data the App does and does not collect.

2. DATA WE DO NOT COLLECT

Cryotrac does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information. Specifically, the App does not collect:

  • Your name, email address, or account information
  • Location data or GPS coordinates
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Contacts, photos, or files from your device
  • Usage analytics or crash reports sent to third parties
  • Any data entered or generated during app sessions

3. DEVICE SENSORS

The App accesses the following device sensors locally on your device only. No sensor data is transmitted externally:

  • Touch / Pointer input — used to animate the signal meter
  • Ambient Temperature Sensor (Android Chrome only) — used to display thermal readings; data stays on-device
  • Speech Synthesis — text-to-speech is handled entirely by your device's built-in TTS engine

4. INTERNET PERMISSION

The App requests internet access solely to load the VT323 display font from Google Fonts on first launch. No personal data is included in this request. The font is cached locally after the first load.

5. THIRD-PARTY SERVICES

The free version of the App may display advertisements served by Google AdMob. AdMob's own privacy policy governs data collected for ad personalisation. You can review it at: policies.google.com/privacy

6. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

Cryotrac is rated Everyone and does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13.

7. ENTERTAINMENT DISCLAIMER

Cryotrac is designed for entertainment purposes only. All sensor readings, detected signals, and generated words are simulated or based on general device inputs and should not be interpreted as scientific evidence of paranormal activity.

8. CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

If we update this Privacy Policy, the new version will be posted at this URL with an updated effective date.

9. CONTACT

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the Google Play Store listing page.
